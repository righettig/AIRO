Welcome to AIRO - Pre-Alpha Version!

Thank you for joining us as we test and refine AIRO. As we’re still in the pre-alpha stage, please be aware that the platform is in active development, and occasional data wipes may occur. This means that your data (such as bot behaviours or progress) may be reset periodically as we test new features, implement changes, or fix bugs.

What to expect:

  • Instability: The platform may experience occasional downtime or slow performance as we make improvements.
  • Bugs: You may encounter unexpected behaviors or errors. We’re working hard to address them.
  • Data Wipes: Your data may be erased from time to time during this early phase. We recommend not relying on the current data for important or long-term purposes.
  • Missing Features: Some features are still under development and may not be fully functional or available.

We appreciate your understanding as we make these necessary updates. Your feedback is crucial to improving the platform, so if you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please report them via feedback form/support link.

Thank you for being part of this early journey with us!

Note: We’ll be making regular updates, so expect frequent changes and improvements. Stay tuned for new features and enhancements!
